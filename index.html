About

I am a brazilian web developer and an "almost" data scientist. I graduated from computer science at the University of São Paulo, Brazil. In 2012, I moved to Europe to do my internship in Hamburg. In 2013 I moved to berlin, where I worked as a web developer for almost 2 years. Convinced that being a data scientist is what I want, I applied and got accepted to the Eramus Mundus Master Program in Data Mining and Knowledge Management (DMKM). After that, I moved to Nantes, France where I did my first year of the Master with the specialization in "Knowledge and Decision". During the summer, I went back to Berlin for doing an internship in Natural Language Processing and Text Mining. Finally, I moved to Alessandria, Italy, where I am enrolled in the third semester of DMKM with the specialization in Relational Data Mining.

I am passionated for challenges, new technologies and pretty designs. On my free time I try to explore the world together with my fiance. I loves my "baby" cat, a good beer, sunny days and tv series.
